My little journey into the world of design

My name is Eugen, and I have been living and working in Chisinau for 18 years. Originally from Orhei, I attended the Faculty of Fine Arts and Design at UPSC University, where I studied four years of fashion design and two years of graphic design. After graduation, I spent a year practicing graphic design. I worked at the period on online advertising campaigns, where I practiced animation and online banner creation. However, my real passion was UI/UX design, and I spent the next year focusing on creating online stores.

This eventually led me to become a product designer, a role I've held for the past four years. As a product designer, I've had the chance to research ways of making projects happen, including generative and qualitative research, developing visual languages, systematizing them into design systems, developing application architecture, prototyping, and more. In my spare time, I pursue hobbies such as photography and illustration creation, which provide an outlet for my creativity outside of work. Aside from my work, and hobbies, I am a proud husband and father of four daughters who bring me immense joy and inspire me to continue pursuing my dreams. Overall, my journey as a product designer has been rewarding and exciting. I look forward to continuing to grow and develop in my role.
