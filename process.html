From Graphic Design to User-Focused Interfaces

I've always been passionate about design and creativity, and I knew early on that I wanted to pursue a career in the field. However, it wasn't until I stumbled upon UI/UX design that I found my true calling. My journey to becoming a UI/UX designer was a gradual one. In college, I studied graphic design, which taught me the fundamentals of design principles, color theory, and typography. While I enjoyed graphic design, I realized that I wanted to create designs that were more interactive and user-focused. After graduating, I began working as a freelance designer, taking on various design projects ranging from logo designs to web designs.
